# FURQAN Academy — Design Context

## Users

**Primary audience:** Global Muslim diaspora (US, UK, Europe, Gulf) seeking online Quran teachers. English-literate but Arabic-valued — the app is bilingual with Arabic as the primary UI language and English as secondary.

**Context:** Students come to FURQAN to find qualified Quran teachers, book sessions (hifz, tajweed, muraja, etc.), attend video lessons, and track their memorization progress. They range from beginners to advanced reciters. Many are non-tech-savvy — the interface must be immediately understandable.

**Teachers** are appointed by admin (no marketplace). They manage availability, confirm bookings, conduct sessions, and log student progress.

**Job to be done:** "I want to find a trustworthy Quran teacher, book a session that fits my schedule, and see my progress over time."

## Brand Personality

**Three words:** Friendly, Accessible, Simple

**Voice:** Warm and encouraging, never intimidating. Islamic in spirit but modern in execution. Speaks respectfully — no casual slang, but not stiff or formal either.

**Emotional goal:** When a student opens the app, they should feel **motivation and progress** — "I can see how far I've come and I want to keep going."

## Aesthetic Direction

**Visual tone:** Clean and modern with Islamic warmth. Dark theme (`#0a0a0a`) with gold (`#D4AF37`) accents — gold evokes Quran ornamentation and premium quality without being flashy.

**References:**
- **Tarteel.ai** — clean, modern, Islamic feel with dark mode
- **Quran.com** — elegant, content-focused, minimal typography
- **Calendly + Notion** — professional scheduling + clean workspace, functional over decorative

**Anti-references:** Avoid cluttered Islamic art patterns, overly ornate calligraphy as decoration, bright/neon colors, or gamification gimmicks. This is not a children's app or a social media platform.

**Theme:** Dark mode only (forced). Gold is the sole accent color — used for CTAs, active states, focus rings, and highlights. Status colors (green/yellow/red/blue) are functional, not decorative.

**Typography:** Geist Sans — clean, neutral, excellent Latin+Arabic rendering. Arabic text is primary and bold; English is secondary in `text-xs text-muted`.

**Layout:** RTL-first. Card-based with generous spacing. Max-width containers (`max-w-4xl` / `max-w-5xl`). Responsive grid (1-col mobile, 2-col desktop).

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness** — Every element must be immediately understandable. If a user needs to think about what a button does, the design has failed. Arabic labels first, English hints second.

2. **Progress is visible** — Students should always see where they are in their Quran journey. Stats, session counts, and quality ratings should be prominent — not buried. Motivation comes from visible progress.

3. **Gold means action** — Gold (`#D4AF37`) is reserved for interactive elements: buttons, links, active states, focus rings. It should never appear on static/decorative elements. When a user sees gold, they know they can tap it.

4. **Respect the content** — The Quran is sacred content. Surah names, ayah references, and recitation terminology must be presented with typographic care. Never truncate Quran references. Session types and recitation standards always show their Arabic names.

5. **Accessible by default** — High contrast (light text on dark bg), large touch targets, no motion-dependent interactions, screen-reader-friendly labels. The global diaspora includes users of all abilities and connection speeds.

## Component Patterns

**Primary Button:** `bg-gold text-black font-semibold rounded-lg py-2.5 hover:bg-gold-hover`
**Card:** `rounded-xl border border-card-border bg-card p-5`
**Input:** `rounded-lg border border-input-border bg-input px-4 py-2.5 focus:border-input-focus focus:ring-1 focus:ring-input-focus`
**Gold Badge:** `rounded-full border border-gold/30 bg-gold/10 px-2.5 py-0.5 text-xs text-gold`
**Status Badge:** `rounded-full border px-2.5 py-0.5 text-xs` + status-specific colors
**Error Alert:** `rounded-lg border border-error/30 bg-error/10 p-3 text-sm text-error`
**Section Heading:** `text-lg font-semibold` with Lucide icon in `text-gold` + `ml-2 inline`
